Hurricane Preparedness
Be ready before the storm
⚠️ Hurricane Warning = Danger is imminent 👁 Hurricane Watch = Possible within 48 hrs
🏠
Secure Your Home
  • Install hurricane shutters or board windows
  • Clear gutters and drains
  • Trim trees and shrubs
  • Bring in outdoor furniture and decorations
  • Reinforce garage doors
  • Know how to turn off utilities
🎒
Emergency Kit (72-hour supply)
  • Water: 1 gallon per person per day
  • Non-perishable food
  • Battery-powered or hand-crank radio
  • Flashlight and extra batteries
  • First aid kit and medications
  • Important documents (waterproofed)
  • Cash in small bills
  • Full tank of gas
🚗
Evacuation Plan
  • Know your evacuation zone (A, B, C, D, E)
  • Plan two routes out of your area
  • Identify nearby shelters
  • Make a plan for pets
  • Don't wait — leave early when ordered
  • Never drive through flooded roads
📱
Stay Informed
  • Sign up for local emergency alerts
  • Monitor NHC advisories every 6 hours
  • Follow local National Weather Service office
  • Download the FEMA app
  • Have a NOAA Weather Radio
  • Charge all devices before the storm
🌊
Storm Surge Awareness
  • Storm surge is the #1 killer in hurricanes
  • Even Cat 1–2 storms can produce deadly surge
  • Check NOAA's surge maps for your area
  • Evacuate surge zones immediately when ordered
  • Move to higher ground, not upper floors
🔋
After the Storm
  • Wait for official all-clear before returning
  • Avoid downed power lines
  • Use generators outdoors only (CO danger)
  • Document damage before cleanup
  • Boil water until cleared by authorities
  • Watch for flooding even after storm passes
